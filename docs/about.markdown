BrainFrame is a personal knowledge management system and universal reader. It
internalizes source material — PDF, EPUB, DOCX, Markdown, archived web pages,
even Linux man pages — and keeps durable cross-links between your notes and the
passages that inspired them.

The goals are simple to state and hard to do well:

- **Read anything**, in one place, on every platform you use.
- **Keep notes linked to their source**, so you can always trace an idea back
  to where it came from.
- **Own your data.** Notes are plain text; source links live in open formats.
  Your library stays on your devices, with the cloud as an option — never a
  requirement.
